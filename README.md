<h1><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>What is a Fire Blanket?</strong></span></h1>
<p>A fire blanket is a fire-resistant sheet, typically made of fiberglass, designed to extinguish small fires by smothering them. It works by cutting off the oxygen supply to the fire, effectively stopping the flames. Unlike fire extinguishers, which require some training and can leave behind chemical residues, fire blankets are simple to use, non-toxic, and clean.</p>
<p>The Prepared Hero Fire Blanket takes this concept to the next level, offering a reliable, user-friendly, and versatile solution for fire safety.</p>
